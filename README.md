# Hi there

I am a Research Associate in Biomedical Computing at [King's College London](https://www.kcl.ac.uk/), working on **medical imaging AI**. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. at the [University of Warwick](https://warwick.ac.uk/) through the [MathSys CDT](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/mathsys/) and the [SIPLab](https://warwick.ac.uk/fac/sci/dcs/research/siplab/), where I worked on efficient and scalable crowd counting, CLIP-based recognition, multimodal driver monitoring, and egocentric action forecasting.

## Research interests

* Robust and generalizable medical imaging AI
* Counterfactual image generation and inter-domain rendering
* Foundation models, representation learning, and multimodal learning
* Domain generalization, OOD evaluation, and model reliability
* Crowd counting, driver monitoring, and human-centric computer vision
